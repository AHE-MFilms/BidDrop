Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 2, 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how BidDrop (operated by America's Home Experts) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the BidDrop platform and homeowner estimate pages hosted at biddrop.us.

1. Who We Are

BidDrop is a roofing estimate and direct-mail platform operated by America's Home Experts. Roofing contractors ("Clients") use BidDrop to create personalized estimates and communicate with prospective homeowners. If you received a postcard or were directed to this page by a roofing company, that company is a BidDrop Client.

For privacy inquiries, contact us at: john@americashomeexperts.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Property address data: Roofing contractors may enter your property address into BidDrop to generate an estimate based on publicly available property records and satellite imagery.
  • Contact information you provide: If you voluntarily submit your name, email address, or phone number through an estimate page, that information is collected and shared with the roofing contractor who created your estimate.
  • Estimate page interaction data: When you visit a personalized estimate page, we automatically collect: the date and time of your visit, whether the page was opened via a QR code, how long you spent on the page, and which buttons you clicked (e.g., "Schedule" or "Call").
  • Device and technical data: We collect your browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your personalized roofing estimate
  • To allow the roofing contractor to follow up with you if you submit contact information
  • To measure how homeowners engage with estimate pages (open rates, scan rates, booking clicks)
  • To improve the BidDrop platform and the quality of estimates delivered
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Advertising Pixels & Tracking Technologies

Estimate pages hosted on biddrop.us may load third-party advertising pixels, including:

  • Meta Pixel (Facebook): Used by the roofing contractor to show relevant ads to people who have viewed their estimate. Meta may use this data in accordance with Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics: Used to measure page performance and ad effectiveness. Google may use this data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

These pixels fire when you open an estimate page and may result in you seeing ads from the roofing contractor on Facebook, Instagram, or Google. You can opt out of Meta ad tracking at facebook.com/settings and Google ad tracking at adssettings.google.com.

5. Direct Mail

BidDrop Clients may send physical postcards to property addresses using a licensed direct-mail service. This is a lawful marketing activity under United States postal regulations. Property addresses used for direct mail are sourced from publicly available county property records and do not require prior consent under current federal law.

To opt out of receiving future mail from a specific roofing contractor, contact them directly using the contact information on the postcard or estimate page.

6. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With the roofing contractor: Contact information you submit is shared with the BidDrop Client who created your estimate.
  • With service providers: We use Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), and Lob (direct mail) to operate the platform. These providers process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • As required by law: We may disclose information if required by a court order, subpoena, or applicable law.

7. Data Retention

Estimate pages are active for a limited period (typically 30–90 days from creation) and expire automatically. After expiration, the page is no longer publicly accessible. Underlying estimate data is retained by the roofing contractor for business record purposes.

If you submitted contact information and wish to have it deleted, email john@americashomeexperts.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the address associated with your estimate.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of targeted advertising

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at john@americashomeexperts.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

BidDrop is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our platform, contact us immediately.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and row-level security on our database. Estimate page URLs contain unique identifiers that make them difficult to guess. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of BidDrop estimate pages after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
