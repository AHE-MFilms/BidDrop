How It Works

Tap a roof. By the time you knock,
they're already expecting you.

From map tap to signed contract — BidDrop handles every step between. You focus on showing up and closing.

01

Tap a Roof on the Map

Open BidDrop on your phone and tap any house on the satellite map. Satellite data measures the roof automatically — owner name, address, square footage, and pitch load in seconds. No clipboard. No knocking.

02

BidDrop Builds the Estimate

A branded estimate page generates in seconds — your logo, your pricing, a live QR code. Every view and scan is tracked so you know exactly when a homeowner is looking.

03

Your Price Lands in Their Mailbox

A real USPS postcard with your estimate, your brand, and a unique QR code ships directly to that address — before you ever reach their door.

04

Call Them Before Anyone Else

BidDrop pulls the homeowner's phone number and flags it as DNC-safe or Do Not Call — so you know exactly who you can call, compliantly, the same day you tap the roof.

05

Automated Follow-Up Fires

Didn't hear back? BidDrop automatically sends follow-up postcards on a schedule you set — 7, 14, 30 days out. No manual work. No leads fall through the cracks.

06

Leads Flow Into Your CRM

Every tapped roof syncs to GoHighLevel (or your CRM) automatically — tagged, filed, and ready to trigger your email drip sequence. Zero copy-paste. Zero missed follow-ups.

07

You Show Up and Close

By the time you knock, the homeowner has seen your postcard, scanned your QR code, and received your follow-up. You're not cold — you're expected.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a credit card to start?
Yes — a credit card is required to start, but you won't be charged a single cent until your first month ends. Cancel anytime before then and you owe nothing.
How does the postcard mailing work?
BidDrop connects to a professional print-and-mail service. When you queue a postcard it gets printed and mailed via USPS — typically arriving within 5–7 business days. Each postcard costs 1 credit ($4 value).
What is Follow-Up Blitz?
Blitz is an automated postcard sequence. You set up 3–5 postcards with different messages, and BidDrop mails them on a schedule you define — 7 days, 14 days, 30 days after the initial knock. It runs automatically without any manual work.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. BidDrop is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — install it on your phone's home screen and it works like a native app, even in areas with spotty signal. The map, pin drops, and estimate builder all work on mobile.
What is GHL integration?
GoHighLevel (GHL) is a popular CRM for contractors. BidDrop's Pro and Agency plans connect directly to your GHL account — every lead you create in BidDrop automatically appears as a contact in your GHL pipeline, with tags and custom fields.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes. You can change your plan at any time from the Plan & Billing page inside the app. Changes take effect immediately through the Stripe Customer Portal.
