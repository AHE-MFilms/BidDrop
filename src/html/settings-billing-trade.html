💳 Subscription & Billing ▾
Manage Subscription opens the Stripe billing portal where you can upgrade, downgrade, update your payment method, or view invoices.
Cancel Plan will cancel your subscription at the end of the current billing period — your account stays active until then.
🏷 Trade-Specific Pipeline Labels ▾
🏷 Customize the pin status labels for each trade. These labels appear on pin cards, filters, and the pipeline view. Leave blank to use the default label.
📬 Trade-Specific Postcard Copy ▾
📬 Set default postcard headlines and copy for each trade. When a postcard is generated for a trade-specific pin, these defaults will pre-fill the Marketing tab fields.
